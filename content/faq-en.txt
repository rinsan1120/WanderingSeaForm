(Q)I want to remove a letter I submitted from Wandering Sea.
(A)Please use the Contact button on this site to send the stationmaster a direct message.
Including the letter ID, title, and sender information will help us process your request smoothly.

(Q)I want to know whether my submitted letter has been published.
(A)The publication status near the top of this site shows the approximate submission date through which letters have been published.
If you submitted your letter before that date and still cannot find it, please contact the stationmaster by direct message.

(Q)I found a typo in my submitted letter and want it corrected before publication.
(A)Unfortunately, the stationmaster cannot determine whether a word is a typo or was written intentionally. Please review your letter carefully before submitting it.

(Q)I want my letter displayed in a specific location in Wandering Sea.
(A)We are sorry, but you cannot request a specific display location.

(Q)I want to find a letter I previously submitted in Wandering Sea.
(A)Use "Find a Letter" on this site to look up the letter ID. Enter that ID into the search system in Wandering Sea to locate the letter.
The interface shown is slightly outdated, but detailed instructions are available in this [YouTube video](https://youtu.be/uoa8-pQY838?si=qIfj0zZn3sSwin7B).

(Q)What languages can be used when sending a letter?
(A)Only Japanese and English are permitted.
