Letters containing any of the following may not be published.

  • Content intended to upset others or containing aggressive language
  • Content or images containing NSFW material
  • Advertising or solicitation for specific services or events
  • Use of real names
  • References that suggest suicide or self-harm
  • Copyrighted works created by others, including lyrics and poetry
  • Negative content about identifiable people or companies, whether virtual or real
  • Other content considered socially inappropriate
  • Impersonation of real people or companies
  • Letters that are already scheduled to be removed shortly after publication

A letter may also be withheld from publication at the administrators' discretion, even if it does not fall under the categories above.
